Chad Alessi - Managing Partner

With an 18-year career spanning oil and gas operations, engineering consulting, and digital marketing, Chad brings a global and practical approach to developing and implementing CX programs for his clients. Leveraging his education in Engineering, Technology, and Data Analytics, Chad focusses on driving measurable and impactful outcomes for his clients.

Prior to NavStella, Chad has held various leadership positions and has lead successful programs at Williams Energy, Kinder Morgan, Aker Kvaener, and NuCor Steel.

Chad holds an MBA in Data Analytics and a Bachelor’s degree in Chemical Engineering from the University of Alabama. He has certifications in Leadership from Harvard Business School, Predictive Analytics from Northwestern University, Full Stack Development from University of Texas at Austin, and is a Certified Reliability Engineer, and IoT Product Manager.

Chad served in the United States Marine Corps and is a proud father of two accomplished and beautiful daughters, and 2-year-old Lab-mix called Teddy.

Although a Crimson Tide fan, Chad is an all-round avid college football enthusiast. He also loves reading, photography, and traveling. A keen advocate of women’s and children’s rights, Chad volunteers and participates in programs and projects supporting women and children.

An avid writer and sharer of knowledge, Chad is a contributing writer to Medium.
